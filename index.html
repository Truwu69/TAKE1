About Us

TAKE1 was formed in 2025 when five friends with a shared passion for rock music decided to create something extraordinary. Drawing inspiration from classic rock legends and modern rock innovators, we set out to create a sound that honors the past while pushing boundaries into the future.

Known for our high-energy live performances and authentic connection with fans, TAKE1 continues to grow our dedicated fanbase across the country. We're committed to keeping the spirit of rock alive while constantly evolving our sound.

Whether you're a longtime fan or just discovering our music, thank you for being part of our journey. Rock on!
